About Us

Built on panels, paint and plain dealing

Camp Rd Auto Services has spent almost two decades turning damaged cars back into ones owners are proud to drive — one honest quote at a time.

Our Story

Started in a two-bay garage on Camp Rd

Camp Rd Auto Services opened with a single goal: give local drivers a panel shop they could trust completely, whether they were paying out of pocket or going through insurance. What began as a two-bay garage has grown into a full panel and paint facility, but the standard hasn't moved — every car leaves looking the way it should have before the damage happened.

Today our team handles everything from minor dent repairs to full chassis realignments and complete resprays, working alongside every major insurer as an approved repairer.

Our Mission

Repair it properly, explain it clearly

Our mission is simple: restore every vehicle to a safe, factory-standard finish, and never let a customer feel confused about what's being done to their car or why. That means itemised quotes, straight answers, and repairs built to last — not just to pass inspection.

We measure success in repeat customers and insurer referrals, not in upsells.
