Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

Do you deal directly with my insurance company?+

Yes. We're an approved repairer for all major insurers and can liaise directly with your claims adjuster, submit quotes, and manage approvals so you don't have to chase anything.

How long does a typical panel repair take?+

Minor panel repairs can be completed in 1–3 days. Larger repairs involving structural work or a full respray typically take 5–10 working days, and we'll give you a firm estimate after assessment.

Can you match my exact paint colour?+

Yes — we use computerised colour matching against your vehicle's factory paint code, including metallics and pearls, so the repaired panel blends seamlessly.

Do I need to pay anything if it's an insurance claim?+

In most approved claims you'll only pay your policy excess directly to us; the rest is invoiced to your insurer. We'll confirm exact costs before any work begins.

Is a courtesy car available while mine's being repaired?+

Courtesy vehicles are available for eligible repairs, subject to availability. Let us know when booking your quote and we'll confirm options.

Do repairs come with a warranty?+

Every repair is covered by our lifetime workmanship warranty for as long as you own the vehicle, covering panel work, paint and rust treatment.
